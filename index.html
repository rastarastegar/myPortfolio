About Me

Reza is an entrepreneur who has in the past built two companies. One was an online tutoring company which matched tutors with students in the nearest coffeeshop. He also built a lab which tested samples from oil wells and with different X-ray methods and pattern analysis it could identify the type of the sample and draw possible conclusions on operation events. Recently, he has returned to coding world in pursuit of realization and commercialization of solutions he with his team has found to some world problems in the areas of home improvement, energy, social behavior and human interaction.
